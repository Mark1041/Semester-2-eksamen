The thoughts behind Come 2 Copenhagen is to educate you as a tourist, so that you can get the full experience and get the most out of your time in Copenhagen. Therefore we have gathered a lot of information for you, so you can plan your trip from what you find most interesting.

Frequently asked questions (FAQs)



How do i get to and from the airport?

The metro is located at the end of the airport arrival hall and will take you to straight to the city centre. The metro runs every 4 minutes and only takes around 15 minutes.


How do i get pay for my ticket to the bus, metro or train?

If you want to travel with bus you can always pay for the ticket on the bus, the metro and train you can either buy it from the station kiosks or the machine on the station. but you need to buy the tickets before boarding the bus, metro and train


I only have a day in Copenhagen. What should i do?

The combination of a 15 minutes metro rides from the airport to the city centre, and a capital small enough to experience by foot means you can get a lot out of even a short stay in Copenhagen.
In one day you can see several neighbourhoods. On foot, by renting a bike or traveling with the metro.
